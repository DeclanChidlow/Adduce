# Donate

If you like Adduce and would like to support it's development then you can support financially or contribute in other ways.

## Financially
You can donate to the main developers and maintainers by going to their websites.

- [ToastXC](https://toastxc.xyz)
- [Vale](https://vale.rocks/donate)

## Contributing
If you aren't in a position to financially support development then contributions are appreciated. You can head over to the Adduce [GitHub repo](https://github.com/DeclanChidlow/Adduce) and checkout what needs to be worked on. The open issues are a good place to start.
